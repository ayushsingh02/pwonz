Greenland Heaven in Mackenzie

Ashwick Flat, Mackenzie, Canterbury

See all photos  

BRING THE FAMILY, THE DOGS AND THE RODS! Totara Peak Station is one of five high country sheep stations which makes up the Benmore Range. It boasts 4,900 hectares of rocky outcrops, scrub and pasture and is home to approximately 3,000 merino sheep and some cattle. Hunt the feral pigs and take in a spot of fishing. Benmore Views Family Camp includes cooking, bathroom and shower facilities.
